Mission

FOR A FREE PALESTINE! END THE GENOCIDE!

The organization of cultural events has frequently been used by the State of Israel to cleanse its international image and, consequently, whitewash its responsibility for the genocide.

In the recent past, we have seen that the collective boycott of Zionist organizations has proven effective in destroying this whitewashing strategy. It serves as a warning to prevent similar situations from happening again. The Anta Gathering has been canceled. We all win – those who love electronic music, the local population, and above all, and most importantly – the palestinian people!

A group of more than twenty people spread across the country—most of them involved in music—decided to join forces to create Liberation Station, which presents itself as a denunciation of any and all Zionist image-washing strategies through music; a humanitarian and entirely voluntary alternative with a strong commitment to raising funds for the cause at hand.

Nine events will be held in five different locations from September 30th to October 5th. The entire program will be streamed, and most events will be held publicly.

All proceeds will go to families in Gaza in need. We've identified four families in urgent need, and for every additional €100, we'll help one more.

We're also partnering with Occupy For Gaza and Coimbra Pela Palestina.

FAQ

Is Israel committing genocide in Palestine?

Yes. According to the United Nations; manyPalestinian and Israeli historians; the International Association of Genocide Scholars; human rights organizations ranging from Al Mezan and PCHR to Amnesty International; the Israeli-based groups B’Tselem and Physicians for Human Rights; governments from South Africa to Saudi Arabia; and countless other researchers and scholars. These charges follow years of apartheid within Israel and endless Israeli efforts to  continue building illegal settlements in the Occupied West Bank, making Palestinian statehood all but impossible. They have not been shy about this.

Artists, venues and collectives coming together to raise their voices against the ongoing genocide of the Palestinian people.

A group of more than twenty people spread across the country—most of them involved in music—decided to join forces to create Liberation Station, which presents itself as a denunciation of any and all Zionist image-washing strategies through music; a humanitarian and entirely voluntary alternative with a strong commitment to raising funds for the cause at hand.

WHAT MOTIVATED THIS INITIATIVE?

The genocide of a people – the palestinian people. The Zionist state’s attempts at whitewashing will not pass. Nor will the whitewashing by platforms like HÖR Berlin. The Weekender Hör Lisboa is not neutral – it is an affront to the palestinian people. That event is the spark behind our initiative, which runs in parallel, on the same dates, using the same channels: music, club culture, streaming and collaborations with community radios. This is what we’re planning across the country – all for one cause: Freedom for Palestine.

WHERE ARE YOUR DONATIONS GOING TO?

Every euro donated to Liberation Station goes to Palestinian families in need. Children in Gaza are being buried under rubble, starved by siege, maimed by bombs. Schools and playgrounds have become targets and sites of trauma. A generation is being erased before our eyes. In the midst of this horror Palestinian humanitarians risk their lives daily to save others delivering aid, food, water, medicine and education. Their work provides hope in the darkness. They lost everything. Give what you can.
